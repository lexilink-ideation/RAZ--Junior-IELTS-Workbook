Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
❤️love(v.)
爱▾
Simple meaning
to like something or someone very much
Example
Dad loves the turkey at dinner.
🦃turkey(n.)
火鸡▾
Simple meaning
a large bird that people cook and eat for special meals
Example
Dad loves the turkey at our family dinner.
🥧pie(n.)
馅饼▾
Simple meaning
a food baked in a round dish, with a crust on top and a sweet or savory filling inside
Example
Mom loves the pie at Thanksgiving.
🥔potato(n.)
土豆▾
Simple meaning
a round vegetable that grows under the ground with a brown skin
Example
I love the potatoes we make together.
🔢count(v.)
数▾
Simple meaning
to say numbers one by one to find out how many there are
Example
I count out the potatoes one by one.
🔪peel(v.)
削皮▾
Simple meaning
to take off the outside skin of a fruit or vegetable
Example
Dad peels the potatoes in the sink.
🚰sink(n.)
水槽▾
Simple meaning
the bowl in a kitchen or bathroom where water comes out of a tap for washing
Example
We wash the vegetables in the sink.
♨️boil(v.)
煮▾
Simple meaning
to heat water or food until it is very hot and bubbles appear
Example
We boil the potatoes to make them soft.
🧸soft(adj.)
软的▾
Simple meaning
easy to press or cut; not hard
Example
The boiled potatoes are soft and easy to mash.
🥣mash(v.)
捣烂▾
Simple meaning
to press food firmly until it becomes smooth and soft
Example
We mash the potatoes together as a family.

📐 Key Grammar

Simple Present: Dad loves, We boil, I count — describing actions now
Plural nouns: potato → potatoes
Infinitive of purpose: We boil them to make them soft

💭 Things to Remember

Each family member loves a different food.
The steps to make mashed potatoes: count → peel → boil → mash → eat!
The family works together to make food they all love.
