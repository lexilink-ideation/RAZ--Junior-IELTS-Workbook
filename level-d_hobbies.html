Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🎯hobby(n.)
爱好▾
Simple meaning
something you like to do for fun in your free time
Example
Different people have different hobbies.
😌relax(v.)
放松▾
Simple meaning
to rest and feel calm
Example
Hobbies help people relax and have fun.
😊enjoy(v.)
享受；喜欢▾
Simple meaning
to like doing something very much
Example
Some people enjoy dancing.
💃dancing(n./v.)
跳舞▾
Simple meaning
moving your body to music
Example
Some people enjoy dancing. They like to move their bodies.
🪙collecting(n./v.)
收集▾
Simple meaning
gathering and keeping many things of the same kind
Example
Some people enjoy collecting. They like to collect different things.
🌱gardening(n./v.)
园艺▾
Simple meaning
growing and taking care of plants
Example
Some people enjoy gardening. They like to grow plants.
✍️writing(n./v.)
写作▾
Simple meaning
putting words on paper to tell stories or ideas
Example
Some people enjoy writing. They like to tell stories.
🎵singing(n./v.)
唱歌▾
Simple meaning
making music with your voice
Example
Some people enjoy singing. They like to make music.
🎨painting(n./v.)
绘画▾
Simple meaning
making pictures using paint or colors
Example
Some people enjoy painting. They like to create art.

Speaking

Practice speaking out loud! 大声说出来！

🟢 Level 1 — Simple Answers

🎤What is a hobby?
🎤Do you enjoy dancing?
🎤What hobby do you like?

🟡 Level 2 — Short Sentence Answers

🎤Name two hobbies from the book. Use: "Some people enjoy ___."
🎤What do people who enjoy painting like to do?
🎤Why do people have hobbies? Use the word "relax".

🔵 Level 3 — Open-Ended Questions

🎤What is your hobby? Why do you enjoy it?
🎤Which hobby from the book would you like to try? Why?
🎤What hobby would you recommend to a friend? Why?
Useful phrases: My hobby is... | I enjoy... because... | I like to... | In my free time, I...
