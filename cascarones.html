Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🥚cascarones(n.)
彩蛋壳▾
Simple meaning
painted eggshells filled with confetti, used for celebrations
Example
We made cascarones for the party by painting eggshells and filling them with confetti.
🎨painted(adj.)
彩绘的▾
Simple meaning
covered with colors or pictures using paint
Example
Cascarones are painted many ways — with faces, animals, and fruits.
🐣eggshells(n.)
蛋壳▾
Simple meaning
the hard outside part of an egg
Example
We saved the eggshells after breakfast to make cascarones.
🎉celebrate(v.)
庆祝▾
Simple meaning
to do something fun and special for a happy event
Example
Cascarones help people celebrate in Mexico and the United States.
🎊confetti(n.)
彩纸屑▾
Simple meaning
small pieces of colored paper that people throw at parties and celebrations
Example
Fill the egg with confetti before you paint it.
💥crack(v.)
打破▾
Simple meaning
to break something open so that it splits apart
Example
People crack cascarones over their friends' heads at celebrations.
🫙fill(v.)
装满▾
Simple meaning
to put something inside a container until it is full
Example
Fill the egg with confetti to make a cascarón.
👆poke(v.)
戳▾
Simple meaning
to push with a finger or pointed tool to make a small hole
Example
First, poke a hole in an eggshell so you can take the egg out.
😂laugh(v.)
笑▾
Simple meaning
to make a happy sound when something is funny or fun
Example
Cascarones make people laugh when confetti falls on their heads.
🍀luck(n.)
运气▾
Simple meaning
when good things happen to you by chance, not because you worked for them
Example
People say cascarones bring good luck!
