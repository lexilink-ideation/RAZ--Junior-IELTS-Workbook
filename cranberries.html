Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🍒cranberry(n.)
蔓越莓▾
Simple meaning
a small, round, red fruit that tastes sour; it grows in wet places
Example
Cranberries are red and taste tart.
🍎fruit(n.)
水果▾
Simple meaning
a part of a plant that you can eat; it usually has seeds inside
Example
Cranberries are fruits. Apples are fruits too.
💧bog(n.)
沼泽地▾
Simple meaning
a soft, wet area of land where special plants grow; like a muddy field
Example
Cranberries grow in bogs filled with water.
🌿vine(n.)
藤蔓▾
Simple meaning
a long plant that creeps along the ground or climbs up things
Example
Cranberries grow on vines close to the ground.
🌸flower(n.)
花朵▾
Simple meaning
the colorful part of a plant; flowers help plants make fruit
Example
Cranberry plants grow pink flowers before making fruit.
😖tart(adj.)
酸的▾
Simple meaning
having a sharp, sour taste that makes your mouth pucker
Example
Cranberries taste very tart — not sweet like candy!
🌱fresh(adj.)
新鲜的▾
Simple meaning
just picked or grown; not cooked, dried, or preserved
Example
People can eat cranberries fresh, right after picking.
☀️dried(adj.)
晒干的▾
Simple meaning
with the water removed by heat; dry and chewy like a raisin
Example
People eat dried cranberries as a snack.
🍯jam(n.)
果酱▾
Simple meaning
a thick, sweet spread made from cooked fruit; you put it on bread
Example
I like to spread cranberry jam on my toast.
🥣sauce(n.)
酱汁▾
Simple meaning
a thick liquid made from cooked fruit or vegetables; poured on food
Example
We eat cranberry sauce with turkey at Thanksgiving.
