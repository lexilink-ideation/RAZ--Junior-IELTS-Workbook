Vocabulary

Tap each card to see the Chinese meaning, a simple definition, and an example sentence. 点击单词卡查看意思！
🧑‍🎨 glassblower(n.)
吹玻璃师 ▾
Simple meaning
a person who makes things out of hot, melted glass
Example
I am a glassblower. Watch me make a beautiful glass bowl.
🔥 oven(n.)
炉子 ▾
Simple meaning
a very hot place that heats things up
Example
The oven that holds the glass is very, very hot.
🍯 runny(adj.)
流动的 ▾
Simple meaning
thin and able to flow, like a liquid
Example
The hot glass is runny, like honey.
🥖 pipe(n.)
管子 ▾
Simple meaning
a long, hollow tube used to blow glass
Example
I dip my long pipe into the hot glass.
💧 dip(v.)
蘸 ▾
Simple meaning
to put something into a liquid for a short time
Example
I dip my long pipe into the hot glass.
🔄 roll(v.)
滚动 ▾
Simple meaning
to turn something over and over while it moves
Example
I roll the pipe on the table to smooth and cool the glass.
✨ smooth(v./adj.)
把…变圆滑 ▾
Simple meaning
to make something even, with no bumps
Example
I roll the pipe on the table to smooth and cool the glass.
🥣 bowl(n.)
碗 ▾
Simple meaning
a round dish, often used for food
Example
My beautiful bowl is done.
🟢 gooey(adj.)
胶状的 ▾
Simple meaning
soft, thick, and sticky
Example
I blow a bubble in the gooey glass.
🔺 shape(v.)
塑形 ▾
Simple meaning
to give something a form by working on it
Example
My tools help me to shape the glass.
🌀 spin(v.)
旋转 ▾
Simple meaning
to turn around and around quickly
Example
I spin the glass as fast as I can.
🌡️ heat(v.)
加热 ▾
Simple meaning
to make something hot
Example
I heat the glass again to make it smooth.

🆕 New Phrases 新学的词组

💨 blow into
吹进去 ▾
Simple meaning
to push air into something with your mouth
Example
I blow into the pipe.
📏 get wider and wider
变得越来越宽 ▾
Simple meaning
to slowly become bigger across, more and more
Example
The bowl gets wider and wider.
🙂 try to do sth
试着做某事 ▾
Simple meaning
to make an effort to do something
Example
I try to make the bowl smooth.
🙅 try not to do sth
试着不做某事 ▾
Simple meaning
to make an effort to avoid doing something
Example
I try not to blow too hard.
⚡ as fast as one can
尽快 ▾
Simple meaning
doing something at the fastest speed possible for you
Example
I spin the glass as fast as I can.

Reading Comprehension

Answer the questions about the story.

A · True or False 判断对错

1.A glassblower makes things out of cold glass.
2.The oven is very, very hot.
3.The glassblower rolls the pipe to smooth and cool the glass.
4.The glassblower tries to blow as hard as possible.
5.The glass cools slowly at the end.

B · Multiple Choice 选择题

1.What does "runny" mean?
2.What does the glassblower use to blow the glass?
3.What helps the glassblower shape the glass?
4.What happens to the bowl when the glassblower spins the glass?
5.Why does the glassblower heat the glass again at the end?

C · Short Answer 简答题

1.What is the glass like when it is hot?

Hint: The hot glass is runny, like honey.
2.Why does the glassblower try not to blow too hard?

Hint: The glass is gooey, so blowing too hard could break the bubble or make the shape wrong.
3.What are the steps the glassblower follows to make the bowl? Name three.

Hint: dip the pipe in the oven, blow a bubble, add colors, shape the glass, spin it, heat it again to make it smooth.
