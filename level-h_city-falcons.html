Vocabulary

Tap each card to see the Chinese meaning, a simple definition, and an example sentence. 点击单词卡查看意思！
🦅 falcon(n.)
猎鹰 ▾
Simple meaning
a fast bird that hunts other animals
Example
Fran and Fred are city falcons.
🐣 chick(n.)
小鸡 / 幼鸟 ▾
Simple meaning
a baby bird
Example
They had three hungry chicks in the nest.
🐦 prey(n.)
猎物 ▾
Simple meaning
an animal that is hunted for food
Example
They needed to find prey to feed them.
🏠 nest box(n.)
巢箱 ▾
Simple meaning
a box people build so animals can live and raise babies safely
Example
Fran and Fred looked down from their nest box on the bridge.
😋 hungry(adj.)
饥饿的 ▾
Simple meaning
wanting or needing food
Example
Fran and Fred had three hungry chicks in their nest.
📷 webcam(n.)
网络摄像头 ▾
Simple meaning
a camera that shows live pictures on the internet
Example
They watched the falcons on a webcam.
⬇️ stoop(n./v.)
俯冲 ▾
Simple meaning
a very fast dive a falcon makes to catch prey
Example
Fran made a fast dive, or stoop, to catch a bird.

Reading Comprehension

Answer the questions about the story.

A · True or False 判断对错

1.Fran and Fred built their nest box on a mountain.
2.People watched the falcons on a webcam and from a park.
3.Fran and Fred had three hungry chicks.
4.A "stoop" is a slow, gentle landing.
5.The chicks grew big enough to fly and stoop after a few weeks.

B · Multiple Choice 选择题

1.Where did Fran and Fred build their nest?
2.How many chicks did Fran and Fred have?
3.What does "prey" mean?
4.What did Fran do to catch a bird?
5.How did people watch Fran and Fred?

C · Short Answer 简答题

1.Why did people build a nest box for the falcons?

Hint: so the falcons could have a safe nest / so people could keep them safe.
2.What did Fran and Fred need to find for their hungry chicks?

Hint: They needed to find prey to feed them.
3.What happened after the chicks grew big enough to fly?

Hint: All the falcons flew away from the nest box, and people waited a year for Fran and Fred to return.
