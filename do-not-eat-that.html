Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🍴eat(v.)
吃▾
Simple meaning
to put food in your mouth and swallow it
Example
Zots wants to eat everything he finds!
🌌space(n.)
太空▾
Simple meaning
the area far above the Earth, where the stars are
Example
Zots is from outer space. He is not from Earth!
🔍finds(v.)
找到▾
Simple meaning
to see or discover something
Example
Zots finds puppies. He wants to eat them!
🗑️trash(n.)
垃圾▾
Simple meaning
things that are not wanted and are thrown away
Example
"Do not eat the trash!" Abby says.
😋hungry(adj.)
饿的▾
Simple meaning
feeling that you want to eat food
Example
Zots is hungry. He wants to eat something!
🌳tree(n.)
树▾
Simple meaning
a tall plant with a trunk and branches
Example
Zots finds a tree. "Do not eat the tree!" Abby says.
👫friends(n.)
朋友▾
Simple meaning
people who like each other and spend time together
Example
Abby and Zots are best friends.
🐶puppies(n.)
小狗▾
Simple meaning
very young, baby dogs
Example
Zots finds puppies. "Do not eat the puppies!" Abby says.
🍕pizza(n.)
披萨▾
Simple meaning
a round, flat food made with bread, tomato, and cheese
Example
"Eat some pizza," Abby says. Now Zots is happy!
🚀outer(adj.)
外部的▾
Simple meaning
on the outside; far away from the centre
Example
Zots is from outer space — very far from Earth!

🧩 Sentence Patterns

"Do not eat the ___!" Abby says.

📐 Key Grammar

Commands: "Do not eat the ___!" — telling someone not to do something
Simple present: "Zots finds..." / "Abby says..." — actions happening now
Adjectives: "hungry" (how you feel) / "outer" (which one) — describing words

💭 Things to Remember

Zots is an alien from outer space. He doesn't know what food is!
Abby is Zots's best friend. She helps him by saying "Do not eat that!"
At the end, Abby tells Zots to eat pizza. Problem solved! 🍕
