Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🐝bee(n.)
蜜蜂▾
Simple meaning
a small flying insect with yellow and black stripes that makes honey and helps plants grow
Example
The bee flew from flower to flower in the garden.
🤏little(adj.)
小的▾
Simple meaning
very small in size
Example
Bees are little, but they do a very important job.
💪big(adj.)
大的▾
Simple meaning
large in size or very important
Example
Bees do a big job — they help us get the food we eat.
🧑‍🔧job(n.)
工作▾
Simple meaning
an important task that someone or something does
Example
A bee's job is to help flowers make fruit and seeds.
🍽️feed(v.)
喂养▾
Simple meaning
to give food to someone or help them get food
Example
Bees feed you and me by helping our food grow.
🚫without(prep.)
没有▾
Simple meaning
not having something — if something is missing
Example
Without bees, we would not have many of our favourite foods.
🤝help(v.)
帮助▾
Simple meaning
to do something useful for someone
Example
Bees help bring us some of our favourite foods.
❤️favorite(adj.)
最喜欢的▾
Simple meaning
the one you like most out of everything
Example
Apples are my favorite fruit, and bees help them grow!
🍎apple(n.)
苹果▾
Simple meaning
a round red or green fruit that grows on a tree
Example
Without bees, we would not have apples to eat.
🎃pumpkin(n.)
南瓜▾
Simple meaning
a large round orange vegetable that grows on the ground
Example
Without bees, we would not have pumpkins for soup or pie.

🧩 Sentence Patterns

___ are little, but they do a big job.
Without ___, we would not have ___.
___ help bring us some of our favourite ___.

📐 Key Grammar

Contrast with "but": Bees are little, but they do a big job.
Conditional with "would": Without bees, we would not have apples.
Simple present: Bees feed you and me. / Bees help bring us food.

💭 Things to Remember

Bees are small insects but they do an important job for us.
Many foods — apples, carrots, lemons, cucumbers, blueberries, pumpkins — need bees to grow.
We should say "Thank you" to bees because they help feed people all over the world!
