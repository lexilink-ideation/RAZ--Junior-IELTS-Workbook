Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🤲care(v./n.)
照顾▾
Simple meaning
to look after someone or something and keep it safe
Example
We help teachers take care of the class pets.
🐹hamster(n.)
仓鼠▾
Simple meaning
a small, round, furry animal with big cheeks, often kept as a pet
Example
Our class pet is a hamster. We pet its fur.
🐾pet(n.)
宠物▾
Simple meaning
an animal that people keep at home or school for company
Example
Our classes have class pets.
🐷guinea pig(n.)
豚鼠▾
Simple meaning
a small, fluffy animal with no tail that makes squeaking sounds
Example
Our class pet is a guinea pig. We hear it squeak.
🔊squeak(v.)
吱吱叫▾
Simple meaning
to make a small, high-pitched sound
Example
We hear the guinea pig squeak.
🐰rabbit(n.)
兔子▾
Simple meaning
a soft, fluffy animal with long ears and a small round tail
Example
Our class pet is a rabbit. We watch it eat.
🦎lizard(n.)
蜥蜴▾
Simple meaning
a small reptile with scaly skin, four legs, and a long tail
Example
Our class pet is a lizard. We keep it warm.
🐢turtle(n.)
乌龟▾
Simple meaning
a slow animal with a hard shell on its back that it can hide in
Example
Our class pet is a turtle. We play with it.
❤️love(n./v.)
爱▾
Simple meaning
a warm, caring feeling you have for something or someone
Example
All class pets need love!

Speaking
