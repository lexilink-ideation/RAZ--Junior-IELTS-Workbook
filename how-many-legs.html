Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🐾animal(n.)
动物▾
Simple meaning
a living thing that can move and breathe, like a dog or bird
Example
Many animals have legs to help them walk.
🦵legs(n.)
腿▾
Simple meaning
the body parts an animal uses to walk, run, or jump
Example
A cat uses its four legs to run fast.
🔢many(adj.)
许多▾
Simple meaning
a lot of; more than just a few
Example
Many animals have legs to help them move.
2️⃣two(adj.)
二▾
Simple meaning
the number 2; one more than one
Example
Birds and people have two legs.
4️⃣four(adj.)
四▾
Simple meaning
the number 4; two plus two
Example
Dogs and cats have four legs.
6️⃣six(adj.)
六▾
Simple meaning
the number 6; four plus two
Example
Insects like ants have six legs.
8️⃣eight(adj.)
八▾
Simple meaning
the number 8; four plus four
Example
Spiders have eight legs.
🔟ten(adj.)
十▾
Simple meaning
the number 10; five plus five
Example
These animals have ten legs to walk with.
✋have(v.)
有▾
Simple meaning
to own or possess something
Example
These animals have four legs.
🔢count(v.)
数数▾
Simple meaning
to say numbers in order to find how many there are
Example
Let's count how many legs this animal has!

🧩 Sentence Patterns

These animals have ___ legs.

📐 Key Grammar

Simple Present: "These animals have eight legs." — describes facts about animals
Plural nouns: animal → animals; leg → legs
Numbers as adjectives: "two legs", "four legs", "many legs"

💭 Things to Remember

Different animals have different numbers of legs.
Birds have 2 legs; dogs have 4; spiders have 8.
Some animals like centipedes have too many legs to count!
