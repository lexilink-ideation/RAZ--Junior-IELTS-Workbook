Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🍎feed(v.)
喂食▾
Simple meaning
to give food to a person or an animal
Example
Who would feed the class fish during school breaks?
💡solve(v.)
解决▾
Simple meaning
to find an answer to a problem
Example
Kate and Kat were good at solving problems.
❗problem(n.)
问题▾
Simple meaning
something that is hard to fix or answer
Example
Their class had a problem — who would feed the fish?
🤝help(v.)
帮忙▾
Simple meaning
to do something good and useful for others
Example
Too many kids wanted to help feed Fred!
🔄turn(n.)
轮流▾
Simple meaning
a chance for each person to do something
Example
"Each of you can take a turn," Kat said.
🧠idea(n.)
想法▾
Simple meaning
a new thought or plan in your mind
Example
Kate and Kat had an idea that could solve the problem.
🏖️break(n.)
假期▾
Simple meaning
a time when school or work stops for a rest
Example
Fred would get a new home each school break.
🏫class(n.)
班级▾
Simple meaning
a group of students who learn together
Example
"That's a great idea!" said the class.

Reading Comprehension

Answer the questions about the story.

A · True or False 判断对错

1.Kate and Kat are good at solving problems.
2.The name of the class fish is Fred.
3.Only one child wanted to help feed Fred.
4.Kat said each person can take a turn.
5.The class thought Kate and Kat's idea was great.

B · Multiple Choice 选择题

1.What was the class's problem?
2.Who are the problem-solvers in the story?
3.How many children wanted to help feed Fred?
4.What did Kat say each person could do?
5.Where did Kate say Fred would go each break?

C · Short Answer 简答题

1.Who are the two problem-solvers in the story?

Hint: Look for the two friends whose names sound the same!
2.What was the problem in the class?

Hint: Think about the class fish and school holidays.
