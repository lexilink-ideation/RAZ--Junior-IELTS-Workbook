Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🥬turnip(n.)
芜菁（萝卜）▾
Simple meaning
a round, white-purple vegetable that grows underground; you can eat it
Example
Grandpa pulled a huge turnip out of the ground.
🌱seed(n.)
种子▾
Simple meaning
a tiny thing you plant in the ground; it grows into a plant
Example
Grandpa planted a turnip seed in spring.
🌿sprouted(v.)
发芽了▾
Simple meaning
started to grow and come up from the ground (past tense of "sprout")
Example
In summer, turnip leaves sprouted from the soil.
🐘giant(adj.)
巨大的▾
Simple meaning
very, very big — much bigger than normal
Example
The giant turnip was so big that no one could lift it alone!
✊grabbed(v.)
抓住了▾
Simple meaning
took hold of something quickly and with force (past tense of "grab")
Example
Grandpa grabbed the turnip leaves and pulled hard.
💪pulled(v.)
拉了▾
Simple meaning
used your strength to move something toward you (past tense of "pull")
Example
Everyone pulled together, and the turnip came out!
🧱budge(v.)
（稍微）移动▾
Simple meaning
to move even a tiny bit; often used in "didn't budge" meaning it stayed completely still
Example
The giant turnip didn't budge — it was stuck in the ground!
💥burst(v.)
冲出▾
Simple meaning
to break out suddenly and with a lot of force
Example
The turnip burst forth from the ground with a loud POP!
🫙heap(n.)
一堆▾
Simple meaning
a messy pile; when people fall on top of each other in a pile
Example
Everyone fell backward in a heap when the turnip came out.
🌸spring(n.)
春天▾
Simple meaning
the season after winter; the weather gets warm and plants start to grow
Example
One day late in spring, Grandpa planted the turnip seed.

2.Who came LAST to help pull the turnip?
3.What happened when Mouse helped pull?
4.How did everyone feel when the turnip came out?

C · Short Answer 简答题

1.Who helped pull the turnip? List all the people and animals in order.

Hint: Grandpa, Grandma, Granddaughter, ___, ___, and ___. There were 6 helpers in total!
2.Why couldn't Grandpa pull the turnip out alone?

Hint: The turnip was very ___. It didn't ___ when Grandpa pulled alone.
3.If you were in this story, what would you say to help pull the turnip? What would you do?

Speaking

🟡 Level 2 — Short Sentence Answers
