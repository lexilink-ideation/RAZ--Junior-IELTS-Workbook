Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🏪shop(n.)
商店▾
Simple meaning
a place where you can go to buy things
Example
This shop sells fresh bread every morning.
💰sell(v.)
卖/出售▾
Simple meaning
to give something to someone in exchange for money
Example
This shop sells flowers and plants.
🍞bread(n.)
面包▾
Simple meaning
a food made from flour that is baked in an oven; we often eat it at breakfast
Example
This shop sells bread that is warm and fresh.
🐟fish(n.)
鱼▾
Simple meaning
an animal that lives in water; also a food that people eat
Example
This shop sells fish from the sea.
🌸flowers(n.)
花▾
Simple meaning
colorful parts of a plant that are pretty and often smell nice
Example
This shop sells beautiful red and yellow flowers.
🥩meat(n.)
肉▾
Simple meaning
food that comes from animals, like chicken, beef, or pork
Example
This shop sells fresh meat for cooking.
🧀cheese(n.)
奶酪▾
Simple meaning
a food made from milk that can be soft or hard and has a strong taste
Example
This shop sells many kinds of cheese.
🍎fruit(n.)
水果▾
Simple meaning
a sweet food that grows on trees or plants, like apples or bananas
Example
This shop sells fruit like apples, oranges, and bananas.
🥦vegetables(n.)
蔬菜▾
Simple meaning
healthy food like carrots, broccoli, and peas that grow in the ground or on plants
Example
This shop sells green vegetables like broccoli and spinach.
🔢many(adj.)
许多▾
Simple meaning
a lot of; more than just a few
Example
So many shops sell so many things!

Reading Comprehension

Answer the questions about the story.

A · True or False 判断对错

1.One of the shops in the book sells bread.
2.One of the shops sells flowers.
3.One of the shops sells toys.
4.One of the shops sells cheese.
5.All of the shops in the book sell the same thing.

B · Multiple Choice 选择题

1.What does the first shop in the book sell?
2.What does a flower shop sell?
3.Apples and bananas are types of ___.
4.What is this book mainly about?
5.What is made from milk?

C · Short Answer 简答题

1.Name one shop from the book. What does it sell?

Hint: The first shop in the story sells something you eat at breakfast.
2.What does a fruit shop sell? Name two kinds of fruit.

Hint: Sweet foods that grow on trees — like something red and round, or yellow and long.
3.Which shop from the book would you like to visit? Why?

Speaking

Practice speaking out loud! 大声说出来！

🟢 Level 1 — Simple Answers

🎤What is a shop? (一个地方，你可以在那里买东西)
🎤Name one food from the book. (bread / fish / cheese / fruit / vegetables / meat)
🎤Do you like to go shopping? (Yes / No)

🟡 Level 2 — Short Sentence Answers

🎤Say a full sentence: "This shop sells ___."
🎤Use "many" in a sentence about shops.
🎤Tell me about one shop from the book. What does it sell?

🔵 Level 3 — Open-Ended Questions

🎤Which shop from the book is most useful? Why do you think so?
🎤If you had your own shop, what would you sell? Why?
🎤How are the shops in this book the same? How are they different?
Useful phrases: This shop sells ___ . | I can buy ___ at the ___ shop. | I think... | In the story, the shop sells...

📐 Key Grammar

Simple Present: "This shop sells bread." — describes what a shop does
Demonstrative: "This shop" uses "this" to point to something nearby
Plural nouns: shop → shops; flower → flowers; vegetable → vegetables

💭 Things to Remember

Different shops sell different things — bread, fish, flowers, meat, cheese, fruit, and vegetables.
Shopping is fun! There are many kinds of shops in the world.
Fruit and vegetables are healthy foods you can buy at a shop.
