Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🏃play(v.)
玩▾
Simple meaning
to do something for fun
Example
We play outside with our friends every day.
❤️like(v.)
喜欢▾
Simple meaning
to enjoy something; to feel happy about something
Example
I like to play with my friends.
🏷️tag(n.)
追逐游戏▾
Simple meaning
a game where one person chases others and tries to touch them
Example
We like to play tag in the park — I run fast!
⚾baseball(n.)
棒球▾
Simple meaning
a sport where you hit a ball with a bat and run around bases
Example
We like to play baseball after school.
⚽soccer(n.)
足球▾
Simple meaning
a sport where you kick a ball into a net to score points
Example
We like to play soccer in the big field.
👗dress-up(n.)
扮装游戏▾
Simple meaning
playing pretend by wearing fun costumes and clothes
Example
We like to play dress-up and pretend to be different people.
🏠house(n.)
过家家▾
Simple meaning
a pretend game where children act like a family at home
Example
We like to play house — I am the cook!
🙈hide-and-seek(n.)
捉迷藏▾
Simple meaning
a game where one person counts and everyone else hides; the person finds them
Example
We like to play hide-and-seek behind the big tree.
🎮video games(n.)
电子游戏▾
Simple meaning
games that you play on a screen, like a TV or tablet
Example
We like to play video games on rainy days.
🔥fire(n.)
火▾
Simple meaning
hot, bright flames; fire can burn things and hurt people
Example
We do not play with fire — it is very dangerous!

Speaking

Practice speaking out loud! 大声说出来！

🟢 Level 1 — Simple Answers

🎤Do you like to play tag?
🎤Is soccer a sport?
🎤Can you play with fire? Yes or no?

🟡 Level 2 — Short Sentence Answers

🎤Tell me one game from the book. Say: "We like to play ___."
🎤What do children NOT play with? Say: "We do not play with ___."
🎤Which game do you like best? Say: "I like to play ___ because ___."

🔵 Level 3 — Open-Ended Questions

🎤What is your favorite game to play with friends? Tell me all about it!
🎤Why is it important NOT to play with fire? Can you explain?
🎤What new game would you like to learn? Why does it look fun?
Useful phrases: We like to play ___ | We do not play with ___ | I like to play ___ | ___ is fun!

📐 Key Grammar

"like to + verb": We like to play tag.
"do not + verb": We do not play with fire.
"But" = contrast: Fun things vs. dangerous things.
