Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🌿bamboo(n.)
竹子▾
Simple meaning
a tall grass plant with hard, hollow stems — the main food of giant pandas
Example
Tian Tian eats bamboo every day. He can eat 40 pounds of it!
🐻bear(n.)
熊▾
Simple meaning
a large, strong animal with thick fur and a short tail
Example
All giant pandas are bears, not raccoons.
🌱survive(v.)
存活▾
Simple meaning
to stay alive, especially when conditions are difficult
Example
Without bamboo, giant pandas cannot survive.
🌿wild(adj.)
野生的▾
Simple meaning
living freely in nature, not kept by people
Example
There are only about 1,600 wild giant pandas left in China.
⭐special(adj.)
特殊的▾
Simple meaning
different from others in a good or important way; made for a particular purpose
Example
In China, Tian Tian lived at a special place for pandas.
🌲forests(n.)
森林▾
Simple meaning
large areas of land covered with many trees
Example
Wild giant pandas live in mountain forests in China.
⚖️weighs(v.)
重达▾
Simple meaning
to have a certain heaviness measured on a scale
Example
Tian Tian weighs 275 pounds — that's as much as 34 cats!
🦝raccoon(n.)
浣熊▾
Simple meaning
a small animal with black fur around its eyes and a striped, bushy tail
Example
Tian Tian has black fur around his eyes like a raccoon, but he is a bear.
💚mate(n.)
配偶▾
Simple meaning
a partner animal — the male or female that an animal stays with
Example
Tian Tian and his mate Mei Xiang eat bamboo together.

Story

Read the story. Target words are highlighted. 多读几次！
🐼🇨🇳 A close-up photograph of Tian Tian the giant panda with black and white fur and black patches around his eyes
Pages 3–4
This is Tian Tian. He is a giant panda. Most giant pandas have black and white fur like Tian Tian. Tian Tian has black fur around his eyes. Raccoons have black fur around their eyes too. But Tian Tian is not a raccoon. He is a bear. All giant pandas are bears.
🗺️🌲 A map showing China and the United States, with a mountain forest area where giant pandas live in China
Pages 5–6
Tian Tian was born in China. He now lives at a zoo in the United States. He came to the United States when he was three years old. In China, he lived at a special place for pandas.

China is the only place where wild giant pandas live. They live in mountain forests. There are only about 1,600 wild giant pandas in China.
🎋🐼 Tian Tian eating bamboo stalks at the zoo, surrounded by thick green bamboo plants
Pages 7–8
All giant pandas eat a plant called bamboo. Bamboo grows in the mountain forests in China. More people are cutting down the forests. Without bamboo, giant pandas cannot survive.

Tian Tian eats bamboo. He can eat 40 pounds of bamboo every day. He also eats biscuits. He eats special fruit popsicles, too.
📊🐼 A weight chart comparing a cat, dog, and giant panda; Tian Tian as a baby with his mother Mei Xiang
Pages 9–10
Tian Tian is very big and strong. He weighs 275 pounds. That's as much as 34 cats.

Tian Tian turned eight years old in 2005. He became a father in 2005, too. His baby was born at the zoo. The baby is growing strong like Tian Tian. Tian Tian and his mate Mei Xiang eat together.
🎉🌳 Tian Tian playing with toys and climbing trees at the zoo to stay healthy and strong
Pages 11–12
Tian Tian stays strong by playing. He plays with toys. He also plays in trees. Do you like to play? How is Tian Tian like you?

📐 Key Grammar

Simple Present: "Wild pandas live in China." — states facts
Comparison: "That's as much as 34 cats." — uses "as...as"
Negation: "Without bamboo, pandas cannot survive." — uses "cannot"

💭 Things to Remember

Giant pandas are bears, not raccoons — both have black eye patches.
Only about 1,600 wild giant pandas remain in China's mountain forests.
Tian Tian eats up to 40 pounds of bamboo every single day.
