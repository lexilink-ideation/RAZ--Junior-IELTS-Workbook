Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🛠️make(v.)
制作▾
Simple meaning
to create or build something with your hands
Example
I make a picture with crayons.
🖍️drawing(n.)
画/素描▾
Simple meaning
a picture you make with a pencil, crayon, or pen
Example
I make a drawing of my house.
🎩hat(n.)
帽子▾
Simple meaning
something you wear on top of your head
Example
I make a hat out of paper.
🌸flower(n.)
花▾
Simple meaning
a pretty plant with colourful petals
Example
I make a flower from paper and glue.
⛓️chain(n.)
链条/纸链▾
Simple meaning
rings or loops linked together in a long line
Example
I make a paper chain with coloured rings.
✈️airplane(n.)
飞机▾
Simple meaning
a machine with wings that flies in the sky
Example
I fold paper to make an airplane.
🎨painting(n.)
画/绘画▾
Simple meaning
a picture made with paint and a brush
Example
I make a painting of the sky.
🌀mess(n.)
杂乱/脏乱▾
Simple meaning
when things are dirty, untidy, or all mixed up
Example
There is paint everywhere! I make a mess!
🖌️art(n.)
美术/艺术▾
Simple meaning
drawing, painting, and making beautiful things
Example
I love art. I like to draw and paint.
❤️love(v.)
喜爱▾
Simple meaning
to like something very, very much
Example
I love art class!

Reading Comprehension

Answer the questions about the story.

A · True or False 判断对错

1.The child makes a drawing in art class.
2.The child makes a car in art class.
3.The child makes a flower.
4.The child makes a painting with paint and a brush.

B · Multiple Choice 选择题

1.What does the child make FIRST in the story?
2.What does the child use to make a painting?
3.Which thing the child makes can fly?
4.What does the child make at the end of the story?

C · Short Answer 简答题

1.Name TWO things the child makes in art class.

Hint: drawing, hat, flower, chain, airplane, painting
2.What does the child make that goes on your head?

Speaking

Practice speaking out loud! 大声说出来！

🟢 Level 1 — Simple Answers

🎤What is art class?
🎤Can you name three things the child makes in the story?
🎤Do you like to draw or paint?

🟡 Level 2 — Short Sentence Answers

🎤Tell me what the child makes. Use "I make a ___." or "The child makes a ___."
🎤What is your favourite thing to make in art class? Say a full sentence.
🎤Describe the mess the child makes. What happened?

🔵 Level 3 — Open-Ended Questions

🎤Why do you think the child loves art class?
🎤If you were in art class, what would you make? Why?
🎤How do you feel when you make something and it is messy? Is that okay?
Useful phrases: I make a ___ | I love ___ | In the story, the child makes a ___ | My favourite thing to make is ___

🧩 Sentence Patterns

📐 Key Grammar

Simple Present: I make — for things I do now or always
Articles: "a" + consonant sound (a hat) · "an" + vowel sound (an airplane)
Exclamation mark (!): shows strong feeling — "I love art class!"

💭 Things to Remember

Making things can get messy — and that is okay!
