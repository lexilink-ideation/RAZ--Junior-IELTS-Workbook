Vocabulary

🚇tunnel(n.)
隧道▾
Simple meaning
a long, hollow passage through the ground, a mountain, or under water
Example
Tunnels are long, narrow, covered passages.
📏narrow(adj.)
狭窄的▾
Simple meaning
not wide; small from one side to the other
Example
Tunnels are long, narrow, covered passages.
🛤️passages(n.)
通道▾
Simple meaning
long, narrow ways you can walk or travel through
Example
Tunnels are long, narrow, covered passages.
🚫avoid(v.)
避免▾
Simple meaning
to stay away from something or not do something
Example
Some tunnels go underground. They help people avoid traffic.
🌊underwater(adj./adv.)
水下的▾
Simple meaning
below the surface of the water
Example
Some tunnels go underwater. They help people avoid taking boats.
⛰️underground(adj./adv.)
地下的▾
Simple meaning
below the surface of the ground
Example
Some tunnels go underground. They help people avoid traffic.
✅safely(adv.)
安全地▾
Simple meaning
without danger or harm
Example
They help people walk and bike safely.
🪨hidden(adj.)
隐藏的▾
Simple meaning
not easy to see or find
Example
Some tunnels are hidden. They help people travel in secret.
💧pipes(n.)
管道▾
Simple meaning
long hollow tubes that carry water or gas from one place to another
Example
Some tunnels have pipes. They help people get water.
