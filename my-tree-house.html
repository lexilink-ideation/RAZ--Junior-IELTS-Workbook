🪜ladder(n.)

Simple meaning

Example

🪵wooden(adj.)
木制的▾
Simple meaning
made from wood
Example

🛋️cozy(adj.)
舒适的▾
Simple meaning
warm, comfortable, and nice to relax in
Example
My tree house has a cozy cot where I can rest.
🛏️cot(n.)
小床▾
Simple meaning
a small, simple bed you can sleep or rest on
Example
I like to take a nap on my cozy cot in the tree house.
🪟window(n.)
窗户▾
Simple meaning
an opening in a wall with glass that lets in light and air
Example
My tree house has a little window so I can look outside.
🏡deck(n.)
平台▾
Simple meaning
a flat outdoor platform where you can sit or stand
Example
My tree house has a long deck where I can sit and relax.
🌄view(n.)
景色▾
Simple meaning
what you can see from a place — the scenery around you
Example
My tree house has a pretty view of the garden and the sky.
🤪silly(adj.)
滑稽的▾
Simple meaning
funny and playful in a fun way
Example
My tree house has a silly slide that makes everyone laugh.
🛝slide(n.)
滑梯▾
Simple meaning
a smooth slope you sit on and slide down for fun
Example
I go down the slide when I leave the tree house.

Speaking

🟢 Level 1 — Simple Answers

🎤What is a ladder? Point up or down!

🟡 Level 2 — Short Sentence Answers

🎤Use "cozy" in a sentence. What is cozy in the tree house?
🎤Describe the view from the tree house using the sentence: "From the tree house, I can see..."
🎤Tell me two things the tree house has. Use: "My tree house has a ___ and a ___."

🔵 Level 3 — Open-Ended Questions

🎤If you had a tree house, what would you put inside? Why?
🎤Why do you think the slide in the story is called "silly"? What makes something silly?

🧩 Sentence Patterns

My tree house has a ___ ___.
It is ___ and ___.

📐 Key Grammar

Nouns — things you can see: sign, ladder, cot, deck, view, slide
Adjectives before nouns: a big sign / a cozy cot / a tall ladder
Simple present: My tree house has a wooden door.

💭 Things to Remember

The tree house has many parts: a sign, ladder, door, cot, window, deck, and slide.
You climb the tall ladder to go UP and use the silly slide to come DOWN.
"Cozy" means warm and comfortable — a cozy place makes you feel happy and relaxed.
