Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🍀lucky(adj.)
幸运的▾
Simple meaning
bringing or having good fortune
Example
"This nut brings me good luck," Bonk says.
🌰buckeye(n.)
七叶树果▾
Simple meaning
a shiny brown nut from a buckeye tree, said to bring good luck
Example
Bonk carries a lucky buckeye nut in his pocket.
🔍mystery(n.)
谜案▾
Simple meaning
something that is very hard to explain or understand
Example
"This is a mystery," says Jupe.
🪞dresser(n.)
梳妆台▾
Simple meaning
a piece of bedroom furniture with drawers for storing things
Example
Bonk puts his lucky nut on his dresser at night.
👖pocket(n.)
口袋▾
Simple meaning
a small bag sewn into a piece of clothing to hold small things
Example
Bonk carries a lucky nut in his pocket.
🐿️squirrel(n.)
松鼠▾
Simple meaning
a small furry animal with a bushy tail that collects and hides nuts
Example
Something crawls in the window. It is a squirrel!
🌱acorn(n.)
橡果▾
Simple meaning
a small hard nut that grows on oak trees
Example
Jupe puts an acorn on Bonk's dresser as a trap.
✅solved(v.)
解决了▾
Simple meaning
found the answer to a problem or mystery
Example
"We solved the mystery," says Jupe.
🐛crawl(v.)
爬行▾
Simple meaning
to move slowly on hands and knees, or to creep through a small space
Example
Something crawls in the window.
👁️spy(v.)
悄悄观察▾
Simple meaning
to secretly watch someone or something
Example
They spy from the bushes to watch the squirrel.

Story

Read the story. Target words are highlighted. 多读几次！
🟢 A friendly green monster named Bonk holding a small shiny nut and smiling
Pages 3–4
Bonk carries a lucky nut in his pocket. It is a buckeye nut. "This nut brings me good luck," Bonk says. "It was with me when I found a nickel," Bonk says.
🏆 Bonk standing proudly with a spelling bee trophy, and then placing his nut on a dresser at night
Pages 5–6
"It was with me when I won the spelling bee," Bonk says. One night, Bonk takes the nut out of his pocket. He puts it on his dresser.
😱 Bonk looking at an empty dresser in shock, and then dropping toast on the floor at breakfast
Pages 7–8
In the morning, Bonk's lucky nut is gone! "Oh, no!" Bonk says. That day, Bonk has bad luck. He drops his toast on the jelly side at breakfast. He breaks his best pencil at school.
👾 Three friendly monsters — Lurk, Jupe, and Uzzle — shaking their heads. Jupe looks thoughtful with a light bulb idea.
Pages 9–10
He talks to Lurk, Jupe, and Uzzle. "Did you take my lucky nut?" "No," they all say. "This is a mystery," says Jupe. Then she has an idea.
🌙 Monsters sitting quietly in the dark in Bonk's room. Jupe is awake and watchful while others sleep.
Pages 11–12
That night, Jupe, Lurk, and Uzzle go to Bonk's house. Jupe puts an acorn on Bonk's dresser. They turn off the lights and wait. Everyone falls asleep except Jupe.
🐿️ A squirrel crawling through a window and grabbing the acorn from the dresser
Pages 13–14
Something crawls in the window. It is a squirrel! The squirrel takes the acorn. Jupe wakes up the monsters. "Follow that squirrel!" she says. The monsters follow the squirrel.
🌳 The monsters hiding behind bushes, watching the squirrel dig in the ground. Then they dig and find the buckeye!
Pages 15–16
They spy from the bushes. The squirrel digs and digs. It hides the acorn and runs into the woods. Bonk and the monsters dig, too. They find Bonk's lucky buckeye nut! "We solved the mystery," says Jupe. "That's good luck!" says Bonk.

Reading Comprehension

Answer the questions about the story.

A · True or False 判断对错

1.Bonk keeps his buckeye nut in his pocket every day.
2.Bonk won a spelling bee when he had the lucky nut.
3.Lurk was the one who figured out how to solve the mystery.
4.The squirrel ate the buckeye nut.
5.The monsters dug up the lucky nut from the ground.

B · Multiple Choice 选择题

1.Where did Bonk put his lucky nut before he went to sleep?
2.What bad luck happened to Bonk at school?
3.What did Jupe put on the dresser to catch the thief?
4.Who stayed awake all night to watch for the thief?
5.Who took Bonk's lucky buckeye?

C · Short Answer 简答题

1.Why did Bonk think the buckeye nut was lucky?

Hint: Look at pages 3–5. Bonk talks about two things that happened while he had the nut.
2.How did Jupe's plan help solve the mystery?

Hint: Look at pages 11–15. What did Jupe put on the dresser, and what happened next?
3.Do you have a lucky object? What is it, and why do you think it brings you good luck?

Speaking

Practice speaking out loud! 大声说出来！

🟢 Level 1 — Simple Answers

🎤What is a buckeye nut?
🎤Who took the lucky nut? (a squirrel / a monster / a bird)
🎤Did Jupe solve the mystery? (Yes or No)

🟡 Level 2 — Short Sentence Answers

🎤Why did Bonk have bad luck? Use "Bonk had bad luck because..."
🎤How did Jupe find the missing nut? Use "Jupe put..."
🎤What did the squirrel do with the nuts? Use "The squirrel..."

🔵 Level 3 — Open-Ended Questions

🎤Do you think lucky objects are really lucky? Why or why not?
🎤Jupe was a good problem-solver. What makes someone a good problem-solver?
🎤If your favourite toy went missing, how would you find it? Tell a step-by-step plan.
Useful phrases: This nut brings me good luck. | This is a mystery! | I think... because... | Follow that ___! | We solved it!
