Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🦅eagle(n.)
鹰▾
Simple meaning
a large, strong bird with sharp eyesight that hunts other animals
Example
"I see an eagle looking down at me."
🐉dragon(n.)
龙▾
Simple meaning
a make-believe creature that can breathe fire
Example
"I see a dragon breathing fire at me."
🌬️breathe(v.)
呼吸▾
Simple meaning
to take air into your body and let it out
Example
The dragon breathes fire into the night sky.
🐍snake(n.)
蛇▾
Simple meaning
a long animal with no legs that moves by sliding on the ground
Example
"I see a snake slithering quickly at me."
🌀slither(v.)
滑行▾
Simple meaning
to move smoothly along the ground in a sliding, snake-like way
Example
The snake slithers quickly through the grass at night.
🦊fox(n.)
狐狸▾
Simple meaning
a small wild animal with a pointed nose and a bushy tail
Example
"I see a fox hunting eagles with me."
👑king(n.)
国王▾
Simple meaning
a man who rules a country and wears a crown
Example
"I see a fair king ruling over me."
🦢swan(n.)
天鹅▾
Simple meaning
a large white bird with a long neck that lives near water
Example
"I see a white swan flying away from me."

📐 Key Grammar

Simple Present: "I see a swan." — to describe what you see now
Present Participle (-ing): "flying away," "breathing fire" — ongoing actions
Articles (a / an): "an eagle" (vowel), "a dragon" (consonant)

💭 Things to Remember

Momma Dog and Puppy Dog look at stars and see different pictures.
Each animal does something special: flying, slithering, breathing fire.
The story repeats the same question-and-answer pattern on every page.
