Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🚚truck(n.)
卡车▾
Simple meaning
a large vehicle used to carry things or sell food
Example
The red truck drives down the street to sell food.
🍳kitchen(n.)
厨房▾
Simple meaning
a place where people cook and make food
Example
Each food truck has a kitchen inside.
🏷️sell(v.)
出售▾
Simple meaning
to give something to someone for money
Example
Food trucks sell many kinds of food.
🔥barbecue(n.)
烧烤▾
Simple meaning
meat or food cooked over a fire or grill
Example
This food truck sells barbecue — it smells delicious!
🌊seafood(n.)
海鲜▾
Simple meaning
food that comes from the sea, like fish and shrimp
Example
This food truck sells seafood such as fish and crabs.
🌮tacos(n.)
玉米饼▾
Simple meaning
a Mexican food made with a folded tortilla shell and filling inside
Example
This food truck sells tacos with chicken and vegetables.
👥crowds(n.)
人群▾
Simple meaning
many people together in one place
Example
Food trucks go wherever there are crowds of people.
😋taste(v.)
尝起来▾
Simple meaning
to have a certain flavor; what you notice when you eat something
Example
The food can taste really good!
🍕pizza(n.)
比萨▾
Simple meaning
a round flat bread with sauce and cheese on top, baked in an oven
Example
This food truck sells pizza with tomato sauce and cheese.
🥪grilled(adj.)
烤制的▾
Simple meaning
cooked on a hot metal grill over heat
Example
This food truck sells grilled cheese sandwiches — warm and toasty!

📐 Key Grammar

Simple present tense: "This truck sells tacos." (Subject + verb + object)
Plural nouns: truck → trucks, crowd → crowds
Adverbs of degree: "really good" — using "really" to make meaning stronger

💭 Things to Remember

Food trucks are mobile restaurants — they move to find customers!
Many different types of food can be sold from food trucks — from pizza to tacos to seafood.
Food trucks go wherever there are crowds because that is where they can find the most customers.
