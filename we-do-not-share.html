Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🤝share(v.)
分享▾
Simple meaning
to use something together with another person
Example
We share our toys at school, but not our toothbrushes.
🦷toothbrush(n.)
牙刷▾
Simple meaning
a small brush you use to clean your teeth every day
Example
We do not share toothbrushes.
🥤straw(n.)
吸管▾
Simple meaning
a thin tube you put in a drink to suck the liquid up
Example
We do not share straws when we drink juice.
🧻tissue(n.)
纸巾▾
Simple meaning
a soft piece of paper used to wipe your nose or face
Example
We do not share tissues when we sneeze.
💇hairbrush(n.)
发刷▾
Simple meaning
a brush used to make your hair smooth and neat
Example
We do not share hairbrushes with our friends.
🍬gum(n.)
口香糖▾
Simple meaning
a soft, chewy sweet that you chew but do not swallow
Example
We do not share gum — everyone should have their own!
✏️answer(n.)
答案▾
Simple meaning
what you say or write when someone asks you a question
Example
We do not share answers on a test — that is cheating!
🤫secret(n.)
秘密▾
Simple meaning
something private that you do not tell everyone
Example
We do not share secrets — they are private!

🧩 Sentence Patterns

We do not share ___.
We do share ___!
I use my own ___.

📐 Key Grammar

Negative: "We do not share straws."
Plural nouns: toothbrush → toothbrushes, straw → straws
Auxiliary "do": "We do share Buddy!" (positive emphasis)

💭 Things to Remember

Always use your own toothbrush and hairbrush.
Straws, gum, and tissues are personal — do not share them.
Some things are fun to share, like a pet named Buddy!
