Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
🤸gymnast(n.)
体操运动员▾
Simple meaning
a person who does gymnastics and knows special body moves
Example
Maya is a gymnast who can do many amazing moves.
🙆stretch(v.)
伸展▾
Simple meaning
to reach your arms or legs out as far as you can to make your body ready
Example
I stretch before doing gymnastics to keep my body safe.
⚖️balance(v.)
平衡▾
Simple meaning
to stay steady in one position without falling down
Example
Maya can balance on the beam without falling.
🦵split(n.)
劈叉▾
Simple meaning
a gymnastics move where you slide your legs apart flat on the floor
Example
She did a perfect split with both legs flat on the floor.
🙃handstand(n.)
倒立▾
Simple meaning
a move where you stand upside down on your hands with your feet in the air
Example
Maya can do a handstand and hold it for a long time.
🌉backbend(n.)
后弯▾
Simple meaning
a move where you bend your body backward until your hands touch the floor
Example
A backbend looks like a bridge made with your own body.
🛏️trampoline(n.)
蹦床▾
Simple meaning
a piece of sports equipment with a bouncy surface that lets you jump very high
Example
Maya can jump very high when she is on the trampoline.
🪵beam(n.)
平衡木▾
Simple meaning
a long, narrow piece of wood raised off the ground that gymnasts walk and balance on
Example
The beam is very narrow, so it is hard to balance on it.
🔄flip(v.)
翻转▾
Simple meaning
to turn your whole body over quickly in the air
Example
Maya can flip on the bars and land on her feet.
🧗climb(v.)
攀爬▾
Simple meaning
to go up something high using your hands and feet together
Example
Maya can climb up the rope all the way to the top.
