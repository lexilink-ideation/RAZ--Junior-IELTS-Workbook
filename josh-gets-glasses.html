Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
👓glasses(n.)
眼镜▾
Simple meaning
two round pieces of glass in a frame that you wear on your face to help you see clearly
Example
Josh got new glasses so he could see the board at school.
🙈silly(adj.)
傻的▾
Simple meaning
looking funny or strange in a way that makes people laugh or feel embarrassed
Example
"I will look silly," Josh said when he heard he needed glasses.
🩺doctor(n.)
医生▾
Simple meaning
a person who has studied medicine and helps people when they are sick or need health care
Example
The eye doctor checked Josh's eyes and told him he needed glasses.
🌟need(v.)
需要▾
Simple meaning
to have to have something; to require something because it is necessary
Example
She told him he needed glasses to see better.
🏀basketball player(n.)
篮球运动员▾
Simple meaning
a person who plays the sport of basketball, running and throwing a ball to score points
Example
Some basketball players wear glasses to help them score a basket.
🚒firefighter(n.)
消防员▾
Simple meaning
a person whose job is to put out fires and keep people safe from danger
Example
Some firefighters wear glasses to see better when they fight fires.
🎨artist(n.)
艺术家▾
Simple meaning
a person who creates paintings, drawings, or other beautiful things using skill and imagination
Example
Some artists wear glasses to help them see better when they make art.
✈️pilot(n.)
飞行员▾
Simple meaning
a person who flies an airplane or helicopter and is responsible for the safety of the flight
Example
Some pilots wear glasses to see better and fly safely.
🐾vet(n.)
兽医▾
Simple meaning
a doctor for animals; a person whose job is to take care of sick or injured animals
Example
The vet wore glasses to see better when she cared for the animals.
🛡️safely(adv.)
安全地▾
Simple meaning
in a way that does not cause harm or danger; without getting hurt
Example
Glasses help pilots see better to fly safely through the sky.

3.Some basketball players wear glasses to score a basket.

B · Multiple Choice 选择题

1.Why did Josh say "No way!" when the doctor told him he needed glasses?
2.Who helped Josh feel better about getting glasses?
3.What do glasses help people do?
4.Which job was NOT mentioned in the story?

C · Short Answer 简答题

1.Where did Josh go at the beginning of the story?

Hint: Think about who checks your eyes when you can't see clearly.
2.Name two jobs from the story where people wear glasses.

Hint: The doctor named five different jobs — basketball player, firefighter, artist, pilot, and vet.
3.Do you think glasses are useful? How do they help people? Write 1–2 sentences.

Speaking

Practice speaking out loud! 大声说出来！

🟢 Level 1 — Simple Answers

🎤What did Josh get in the story?
🎤Where did Josh go at the beginning?
🎤Do pilots wear glasses? Yes or no?

🟡 Level 2 — Short Sentence Answers

🎤Why didn't Josh want glasses at first? Use the word "silly" in your answer.
🎤Name one person from the story who wears glasses. Say a full sentence: "Some ___ wear glasses to ___."
🎤What did the doctor say to make Josh feel better? Try to remember one thing she said.

🔵 Level 3 — Open-Ended Questions

🎤Do you wear glasses or know someone who does? How do glasses help that person?
🎤Why are glasses important for a pilot or a firefighter? What might happen if they couldn't see well?
🎤Josh changed his mind by the end of the story. Have you ever changed your mind about something? Tell me about it.
Useful phrases: "Some ___ wear glasses." | "Glasses help them see better to ___." | "I think I'm going to ___." | "They don't look ___."

📐 Key Grammar

Simple Present: "Some pilots wear glasses." — facts that are always true
Purpose infinitive (to + verb): "...to fly safely." — explains why
Plural nouns: firefighters, artists, pilots — add -s for more than one

💭 Things to Remember

Many people in many different jobs wear glasses.
Glasses help people see better — they are useful, not silly!
It is okay to change your mind when you learn new things, just like Josh did.
