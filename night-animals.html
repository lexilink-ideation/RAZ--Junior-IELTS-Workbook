Vocabulary

Tap each card to learn the word! 点击单词卡查看意思！
⚡active(adj.)
活跃的▾
Simple meaning
awake and busy doing things; moving around, not resting
Example
This owl is active at night — it flies and hunts in the dark.
🌑dark(adj.)
黑暗的▾
Simple meaning
with little or no light; the opposite of bright
Example
Night animals can find food in the dark when there is no sunlight.
😴sleep(v.)
睡觉▾
Simple meaning
to rest with your eyes closed and your body still
Example
Night animals sleep during the day while the Sun shines.
⭐special(adj.)
特别的▾
Simple meaning
different from ordinary things; having a particular useful ability
Example
Night animals have special eyes that help them see in the dark.
👁️senses(n.)
感官▾
Simple meaning
the ways your body gathers information — sight, hearing, smell, taste, and touch
Example
Their senses help them find food at night — they can hear and smell very well.
🌸nectar(n.)
花蜜▾
Simple meaning
a sweet liquid found inside flowers that animals and insects love to eat
Example
The bat eats fruit and nectar in the dark at night.
🦅catch(v.)
抓住▾
Simple meaning
to grab and hold something that is moving
Example
The owl catches mice and rats in the dark at night.
🐆hunt(v.)
猎食▾
Simple meaning
to search for and chase animals to use as food
Example
The tiger hunts deer and wild pigs in the dark at night.
🪱worm(n.)
虫子▾
Simple meaning
a small, soft creature with no legs that lives in soil or water
Example
The hermit crab looks for little fish and worms to eat in the dark.
🦪clam(n.)
蛤蜊▾
Simple meaning
a small sea creature that lives inside two hard shells
Example
The nurse shark finds crabs and clams to eat in the dark water at night.

🧩 Sentence Patterns

This ___ is active at night.
It ___ (catches / hunts / finds) ___ in the dark.
Their senses help them find food at night.

📐 Key Grammar

Simple present for facts: The owl catches mice. / The tiger hunts at night.
Infinitive of purpose: special eyes to help them see / to find food
Time clauses: active at night, when it is dark / sleep during the day while the Sun shines

💭 Things to Remember

Nocturnal animals are active at night and sleep during the day.
They use special eyes plus strong hearing and smell (their senses) to survive in the dark.
Night animals can be found in the air (owl, bat), in water (shark, hermit crab), and on land (tiger).
